BiCAPT: Bidirectional Computer-Assisted Pronunciation Training

An end to end style-preserved mispronunciation detection and correction system

Our system detect and correct the mispronunciation speech at the same time with good performance. What's more, we preserve the speech style in the correction generation process, which is better and more efficient for the CAPT systems with different accents.

BiCAPT
HQTV_arctic_a0036: She turned in at the hotel.
Raw Google TTS Glow TTS
Spectrum-based (Monophone) Spectrum-based (Triphone)
BiCAPT (Monophone) BiCAPT (Triphone)

Conclusion

Raw input here is 'She turned into the hotel'. according to the ground truth, our method corrected raw input and generated the right speech 'She turned in at the hotel' with the same speech style. This example shows BiCAPT's ability to correct pronunciation while retaining raw speech style.

For our method, 'Monophone' preserves more style information, making the output more natural. 'Triphone' method sacrifices some style information to achieve better correction performance( lower WER). The results of these two methods both have high qualities and show a trade-off between style information and correction performance.

Compared with all other models, our method is most tiny and slim. 'Google TTS' cannot preserve raw pronunciation style. 'Glow TTS' can only unnaturally maintain partial pronunciation style. 'Spectrum-based' methods have a low quality of generation.
